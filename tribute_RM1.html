About RM

Kim Namjoon, known professionally as RM, is the leader of the global K-pop group BTS. He is widely respected for his intelligence, thoughtful lyrics, and inspiring leadership. RM is not only a rapper but also a poet, writer, and speaker who often talks about self-love, growth, and understanding oneself.

He played a key role in BTS's international success and often represents the group in global interviews because of his excellent English communication skills and deep perspective on life.
