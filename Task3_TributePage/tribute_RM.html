About RM

Kim Namjoon, known professionally as RM, is the leader of the global K-pop group BTS. He is widely respected for his intelligence, thoughtful lyrics, and inspiring leadership.

RM is not only a rapper but also a poet, writer, and speaker who often talks about self-love, growth, and understanding oneself.
